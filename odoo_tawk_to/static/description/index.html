Odoo LiveChat Tawk To

Odoo LiveChat Tawk To allows you to message your website visitors in real time. Tawk to is a free messaging app that allows you to chat with your website visitors

Salient Features

  • Simple and easy to use.
  • Works for both Odoo backend and Odoo Website.
  • Works on Odoo backend or Odoo website or Both on your choice.
  • Communicate with your backend users or sales persons or sellers in real time.
  • Communicate with your website visitors in real time.
  • Exchange files, images etc in realtime.
  • For tawk.to features click here .

Views



View of the tawk.to chatboard in Odoo website.
Use this to communicate with your website users.



View of the tawk.to chatboard in odoo backend.
Use this chatboard to communicate with different sellers or users.



Click on the chatboard window to start the chat.
It will be displayed both in website and odoo backend.
